About Linpane Legal Associates

Founded in 1990 by Adv. A.V. Linpane, our firm has grown to become one of the most respected legal practices in the region. With a legacy of over three decades, we have successfully represented thousands of clients in complex legal matters.

Our approach combines traditional legal expertise with modern strategies, ensuring that our clients receive comprehensive and effective representation. We believe in building strong attorney-client relationships based on trust, transparency, and communication.

At LLA, we handle a wide range of legal matters including criminal defense, civil litigation, family law, corporate law, and property disputes. Our track record of success speaks to our commitment to achieving favorable outcomes for our clients.
